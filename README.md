# Chipul8or

Just drag and drop a rom onto it to play.

Keep in mind that there's no sound yet, and that it still might not work with some roms.

### Key Mappings
1 | 2 | 3 | 4
-- | -- | -- | --
q | w | e | r
a | s | d | f
z | x | c | v
